About Me

Hello! I'm Ron Ritcey, a QA professional with 6 years of experience. I am a dedicated Quality Assurance professional with a strong background in the fintech sector. Over the past six years, I have honed my skills in both manual and automated testing, with a focus on creating comprehensive test plans, designing high-value test cases, and implementing robust automation strategies. My experience includes collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure product quality and reliability across web and mobile applications.

Key achievements include leading QA efforts at fast-paced, high-growth startups, where I developed and presented test plans, executed functional and regression testing, and automated test cases using tools like Cypress and Selenium WebDriver. My technical proficiency extends to programming in Python and JavaScript, and I am skilled in using testing tools such as Postman and TestRail, as well as design and collaboration platforms like Figma, BitBucket and GitLab.

With certifications in ISTQB Foundations and ScrumMaster, I bring a strong foundation in quality assurance best practices and agile methodologies. My commitment to continuous improvement and fostering a culture of quality makes me a valuable asset to any team.
